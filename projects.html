Projects & Goals

         Saving the Earth

   by reducing our carbon footprint

One of the main problems we face on earth is climate change. In our daily lives, we produce greenhouse gases(GHG) that contributes to global warming. In order to try and reduce my own GHG emissions, i have decided to try to cycle to most places instead of using private or public transport. As Singapore is a small city state, it is fairly easy to transition to cycling around as everything is close by.

         Saving the Earth

   by sorting our trash and recycling

Most of our waste ends up in landfills or oceans where they take up space and pollute the environment. We can reduce this by playing our part to reduce the waste we generate. We can make use of the 3 R's. Reuse, Reduce, Recycle. By recycling we can minimise the waste going towards landfills. Its a good habit to start recycling. Start now. Start today.

         Saving the Earth

   Volunteer for non-profit organisations or events

One of the organisations I volunteer at is "Trash Hero Sinapore". They organise events to pick up trash along beaches and the streets of Singapore. This reduces the pollution on our streets and beaches making them cleaner. You can play a part by volunteering at your local events too. Together we can play a part in slowly but surely making the earth clean and free from pollutation again.
